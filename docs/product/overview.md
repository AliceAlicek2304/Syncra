# Syncra Product Overview

Syncra is a workflow automation and multi-channel social media scheduler platform designed for developers, creators, and AI agents. It centralizes posting, inbox management, drip campaigns, and CRM across 14 platforms into a single interface.

## Branding & Visual Language

Syncra features a premium, confident design language inspired by Zapier:
- **Canvas** (`#fffefb`): A warm-cream off-white page background.
- **Ink** (`#201515`): Deep warm coffee-colored text (no harsh pure black).
- **Primary Accent** (`#ff4f00`): Saturated orange color reserved for primary CTAs and conversion hooks.
- **Border Radii**: 12px (`rounded.md`) for cards and buttons, providing a balanced, modern appearance.

## Core Concepts & Entities

- **Profile**: A logical container grouping social accounts together under a single brand identity or project (e.g. "Personal Brand", "Syncra Corporate").
- **Account**: A connected social media integration (e.g. a specific Twitter handle or LinkedIn page) belonging to a Profile.
- **Workspace**: A collaborative workspace environment supporting multi-tenancy for teams.
- **Post/Idea**: Content to publish immediately or schedule for future delivery across multiple platforms.
- **Group**: Content tags or folders inside a workspace to organize posts and ideas.
- **Contact / Channel**: CRM profiles mapping subscriber details (tags, custom fields) across social platform channels.
- **Sequence**: Automatic drip campaign sequences with scheduled delays.
- **Automation**: Comment-to-DM triggers that automatically send a DM or reply when users comment specific keywords.
- **Analytics**: Performance tracking for posted contents (reach, follower count, engagement heatmaps).
- **Billing**: Subscription tier management integrated with Stripe.
